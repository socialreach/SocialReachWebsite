Social Media Marketing

We provide effective and revolutionary social media marketing services. We focus our social media marketing on the following main platforms:

Tiktok Social Media Marketing
On Tiktok, we would create you a Tiktok account(if you don't already have one) and add your website link to your link in your profile. Additionally, we would post 2-3 videos advertising your service/product. This allows your product/service to be exposed to thousands of people daily.
Instagram Social Media Marketing
On Instagram, we would create you a Instagram account(if you don't already have one) and add your website link to your link in your profile. Additionally, we would post 2-3 reels(short videos) advertising your service/product, along with an custom post on your page that is designed by us. This allows your product/service to be exposed to thousands of people.
Facebook Social Media Marketing
On Facebook, we would create you a Facebook account(if you don't already have one) and add your website link to your link in your profile. Additionally, we would make 2-3 daily posts on Facebook. All you have to do is give us the information, and we would use professional software to design the posts ourselves. This creates a innovative and sensory experience with your customers or people interested in your products/services by social media.
Twitter Social Media Marketing
On Twitter, we would create you a Twitter account(if you don't already have one) and add your website link to your link in your profile. Additionally, we would make 2 daily tweets and 2 daily media posts. All you have to do is give us the information, and we would use professional software to design the posts ourselves. This creates a innovative and sensory experience with your customers or people interested in your products/services by social media.
More Social Media
You have the option to choose one more social media app that we would do marketing on. Some options are: Discord, Youtube, and Reddit.

Then, we would email you our weekly statistics on the social media pages, this includes likes, views, comments, follows, and more. This allows full transparency on what we are doing and our progress.

Website Development

For website development there would be three scenarios.

1. No website
If you do not have a website, we would create you a custom website from scratch. You are required to give us all necessary information and a basic overview of what you want your website to look like. Before we start, however, we would give you 3-5 styles that you would need to pick, such as modern, artistic, minimal, etc.
2. Incomplete/severely outdated
If you have a website howeever it is either incomplete, severely outdated, or needs massive work, we would help you redo the website entirely. All you need to do is inform us on what you want on the website and a basic look of the website when its done.
3. Complete website, however needs rework
If you have a complete website, however it needs reworking, we would redo the specific section based on your specifications.

After we have finished your website, we would be maintaining it for as long as the contract goes. We would take unlimited requests for website updates so you can change anything you want anytime. Additionally, we would also provide occasional insights on what we can update to make the website look more appealing to the customer.

Editing Services

We offer various editing services including the following:

1. Posts
In the Editing Services plan, we offer 1 picture based edits per day. This can be for youtube thumbnails, advertisements, or posts. You can choose either they are vector based images or raster images. For every additional advertisement, it would cost 40 dollars. We guarantee the advertisements to be at a professional level of quality.
2. Video Edits
In the Editing Services plan, we offer a 10 minute video edit every 3 days. This can broken up to be 10 instagram/tiktok/youtube short videos, or it can be a large video. For every extra minute, we would charge 20 dollars.
3. Photography
In the Editing Services plan, we offer 1 professionally photographed advertisement per week. We would send professionals to your location to photograph your product. It would then be processed and edited by our editing professionals. For every additional photographed advertisement, it would cost 100 dollars.

If you have any additionally editing requests not specificed here or custom editing plans, you are welcome to contact us for more information.
